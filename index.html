Bake with
Cups & Spoons

When I first started to learn baking, I didn’t have a lot of “tools”. I didn’t have kitchen scale or mixer. I only had a set of measuring cups & spoons bought as a basic starter kit. I often searched on the internet to convert ingredients from various units into cups & spoons.

If you are like me who love baking (and cooking) but don’t want to get all the “bells & whistles” to prepare your ingredients, you can simply measure everything in cups & spoons using our converter. You can also get inspiration from the delicious images on Instagram shared by fellow food lovers and build your own recipes.
